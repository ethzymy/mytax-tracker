Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

At MYTax Tracker, we prioritize your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection & Storage

MYTax Tracker is a privacy-first application. All tax calculations and data inputs are performed locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your financial data to any external servers.

2. Data Usage

The data you enter is used solely for the purpose of tax estimation and generating reports (e.g., Excel export). This data remains in your browser's local storage and is cleared if you wipe your site data.

3. Compliance

We aim to comply with the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) 2010. Since we do not collect personal data, your privacy is maintained by default.

4. Third-Party Services

We use standard Google Fonts and SheetJS for Excel exports. These services do not receive your tax data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy, please contact us at: tax@ethzy.my
