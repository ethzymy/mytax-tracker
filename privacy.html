Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. It is MYTax Tracker's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our application.

1. Information We Collect

MYTax Tracker is designed as a calculator tool that processes data locally on your device.

  • We do not collect personal data: We do not ask for or store your name, email, phone number, or any personally identifiable information on our servers.
  • We do not store financial data: Your income, tax reliefs, and other financial figures entered into the app are stored locally in your browser's LocalStorage for your convenience only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us.

2. Use of Information

Since we do not collect personal or financial data, we do not use, share, or sell your information.

3. Local Storage

We use local storage technology to save your progress so you don't lose your data when you close the app. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser cache/site data.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at: support@ethzy.my
